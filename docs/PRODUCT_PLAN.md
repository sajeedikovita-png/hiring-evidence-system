# Product Plan

## Product Name

Working name: Hiring Evidence System

This is a B2B recruiter and company-facing web application.

## Product Purpose

The product helps recruiters and companies evaluate candidates using job-related evidence.

The system should not claim to choose the best candidate.

The system should help humans understand:

- What the job requires
- What evidence the candidate has
- What evidence is missing
- What needs to be verified in interview
- Whether any unfair or risky decision wording is being used
- What final human decision was made

## Core Product Principle

AI assists.
Human decides.
Evidence explains.

## What This Product Is

This product is:

- A recruiter/company web app
- A candidate evidence review system
- A structured hiring support tool
- A fairness-aware review workspace
- A human decision documentation system

## What This Product Is Not

This product is not:

- A full ATS
- A job board
- A recruitment agency
- A background check company
- An automatic candidate rejection system
- A LinkedIn scraper
- A black-box AI ranking system

## Main Users

### Company Admin

- Creates company account
- Adds recruiters or hiring managers
- Creates jobs
- Manages company settings

### Recruiter / Hiring Manager

- Creates job posts
- Reviews AI-extracted job criteria
- Reviews candidate reports
- Adds notes
- Makes final human decisions

### Candidate

- Opens application link
- Uploads resume
- Adds optional profile links
- Answers job-specific questions
- Gives consent
- Submits application

## Main Workflow

1. Company creates a job
2. Recruiter pastes job description
3. AI extracts job requirements
4. Recruiter reviews and edits criteria
5. System creates application link
6. Candidate applies through link
7. Candidate uploads resume
8. Candidate answers job-specific questions
9. Candidate gives consent
10. AI creates evidence report
11. Recruiter reviews report
12. Recruiter makes human decision
13. System saves audit record

## Main Product Output

The main output is the Candidate Evidence Report.

The report includes:

- Candidate overview
- Job criteria
- Evidence matrix
- Missing evidence
- Questionnaire review
- Suggested interview questions
- Fairness check
- Recruiter notes
- Human decision log
